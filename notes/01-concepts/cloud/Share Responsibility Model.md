Its a policy for what is defined as the responsibility of the Cloud provider and the org/user for security and privacy. 

This policy defines that the **cloud provider is responsible security and privacy of the cloud** infrastructure and its services, meaning that for instance, your cloud database if tagged as private by the user, the cloud provider cannot leak it to other users.

This policy also defines that the **customer is responsible for his/her usage in the cloud** services, meaning that for instance the usage of firewall, encryption of the data, DDoS, etc, are all in the responsibility of the customer and not of the cloud provider.
