Compared to [[Vector#Dot product|Cosine Similarity]] if you centralise the [[Dataset]] you will have the same value for Cosine Similarity to Pearson Correlation. 



$$
r = \frac{\sum_i^n (x_i - \bar{x})(y_i-\bar{y})}{\sqrt{\sum_i^n (x_i - \bar{x})^2} \cdot \sqrt{\sum_i^n (y_i - \bar{y})^2}} \\
-1 \leq r \leq 1
$$

It also can be written as:

$$
r = \frac{\vec{x} \cdot \vec{y}}{\|\vec{x}\| \|\vec{y}\|}
$$
